### Teaching Assistant
[CHEN10040: Introduction to Engineering Computing](https://hub.ucd.ie/usis/!W_HU_MENU.P_PUBLISH?p_tag=MODULE&MODULE=CHEN10040){:target="_blank"}
<p style="text-align:justify;"> Students learned about computers, software, and computer systems in this subject, with a focus on how to use them to solve problems in real life. A organized curriculum taught students how to program in Microsoft Excel<sup>TM</sup> and MATLAB<sup>TM</sup>. This gave them the chance to work on engineering-based case studies and improve their ability to think critically and solve problems. Besides real-life case studies, the course covered data management, algorithm creation, Excel<sup>TM</sup> features such as data analysis and optimization, and the basics of MATLAB<sup>TM</sup> programming, such as vectors, matrices, and user-defined functions.</p>
  
### Research Mentorship
- Introduced master's and PhD students to thin film technologies like spin coating, RF sputtering, e-beam evaporation, and chemical vapor deposition (CVD)
- Provided guidance on computational tools like Linux and shell scripting languages
- Guided master's and PhD students in their early-stage research endeavors
- Introduced students to Gaussian, Nwchem, VENUS, Gromacs, and VASP software packages
